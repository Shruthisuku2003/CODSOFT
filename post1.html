Top 10 Tips for Online Privacy

1. Use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts.

2. Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.

3. Regularly update your software and devices to patch security vulnerabilities.

4. Be cautious when sharing personal information online, especially on social media platforms.

5. Use a reputable VPN service to encrypt your internet connection and protect your online privacy.

6. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.

7. Review and adjust your privacy settings on websites and apps to control the information you share.

8. Be mindful of phishing attempts and never provide sensitive information in response to unsolicited requests.

9. Regularly review your online accounts and remove any that you no longer use.

10. Educate yourself about common online threats and stay informed about best practices for online privacy.
